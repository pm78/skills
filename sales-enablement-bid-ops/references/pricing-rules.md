# TokenShift Pricing Rules

## Price Card (EUR HT)

| Product | List Range | Pioneer Entry | Average |
|---------|-----------|---------------|---------|
| DIAGNOSE | 75K - 150K | 60K (lighthouse) / 75K (pioneer) | 100K |
| BUILD | 500K - 2M+ | 400K (MVP scope) | 750K |
| TRANSITION | 250K - 750K | 200K (pilot cohort) | 400K |
| ASSURE | 10-25K/month | 8K/month (bundled) | 15K/month |

## Discount Policy (Year 1 - Pioneer Phase)
- **Pioneer**: -20% for first 5 clients (case study rights required)
- **Multi-phase**: -10% when signing DIAGNOSE + BUILD together
- **Gov co-funded**: Adjust to match Bpifrance/ESF subsidy levels

## Pricing Decision Tree
1. Is client one of 5 urgency targets? -> Apply pioneer pricing
2. Is client a flagship logo (CAC40, SBF120)? -> Pioneer justified for marquee value
3. Is deal complexity high (multi-country, >10K employees)? -> Price at upper range
4. Is there multi-phase upsell potential? -> Can discount DIAGNOSE to land
5. Is there competitive pressure? -> Floor is lighthouse price (EUR 60K)

## Billing Terms
- DIAGNOSE: 50% kick-off / 50% delivery (or 100% on delivery for small scope)
- BUILD: 30% signing / 40% mid / 30% delivery
- TRANSITION: 20% signing / 40% mid / 40% completion
- ASSURE: Monthly invoicing, 12-month minimum
