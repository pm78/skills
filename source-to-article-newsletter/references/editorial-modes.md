# Editorial Modes

## article

Use for long-form thought leadership.

Target:

- Clear thesis
- Structured subheadings
- Evidence-backed arguments
- Practical implications

## newsletter

Use for periodic update format.

Target:

- Short intro
- Top updates grouped by theme
- Why-it-matters commentary
- One clear CTA or follow-up action

## Shared Rules

- Ground claims in provided sources only.
- Add concise source references.
- Avoid invented metrics or quotes.
- Prefer concise, scan-friendly paragraphs.
