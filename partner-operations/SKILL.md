# partner-operations

## Purpose
Run partner strategy, program setup, and enrollment outreach with execution discipline.

## Use When
- Defining partner segmentation, tiers, incentives, or onboarding model.
- Building or fixing partner recruitment and enrollment workflows.
- Creating partner outreach plans, messaging, and follow-up cadences.

## Workflow
1. Frame objective, target segment, and timeline.
2. Build partner program structure (value exchange, requirements, enablement, KPIs).
3. Create prioritized outreach list and contact strategy.
4. Draft outreach assets and enrollment sequences.
5. Track pipeline health and optimize based on response/conversion data.
6. Delegate parallel research, drafting, and QA to sub-agents when useful.

## Outputs
- Partner strategy brief.
- Program operating checklist.
- Enrollment outreach plan with templates and KPIs.
